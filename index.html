Summary

My name is Nguyen Anh Minh MAI. I'm a Ph.D student in Computer Science at Paul Sabatier University in Toulouse, France. I'm a member of MINDS team of Toulouse Computer Science Research Institute (IRIT) and member of STI team of Toulouse Cerema Research Center (Cerema) in collaboration with an autonomous vehicle company Easymile in Toulouse, France.
Currently i am working on a project about environment perception (3D object detection and semantic segmentation on point cloud from camera & LiDAR sensors) for self-driving cars.

My current research is focusing on scene understanding (shape, depth, motion, object detection and recognition) from images, videos and 3D sensors like LiDAR, Realsense.

Fields of Interest: Artificial Intelligence, Backend Development, Quantitative research, Production ML systems
