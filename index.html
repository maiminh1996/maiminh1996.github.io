Summary

I'am Nguyen Anh Minh MAI. I'm actually a Ph.D student in Computer Science at Toulouse Computer Science Research Institute (IRIT) and Toulouse Cerema Research Center (Cerema) in collaboration with a autonomous vehicle company Easymile in Toulouse, France.
Now, i'am currently working on a project about environment perception (3D object detection and semantic segmentation on point cloud from stereo & LiDAR sensor) for self-driving cars.

My research is focusing on scene understanding (shape, depth, motion, object detection and recognition) from images, videos and 3D sensors like LiDAR, Realsense. I also like to deploy Machine Learning models into production (web/ mobile application, embedded system, etc).
