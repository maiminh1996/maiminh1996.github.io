About me

My name is Nguyen Anh Minh MAI. I'm actually a Ph.D student in Computer Science at Toulouse Computer Science Research Institute (IRIT) and Toulouse Cerema Research Center (Cerema) in collaboration with a autonomous vehicle company Easymile in Toulouse, France.
My research is focusing on scene understanding (shape, depth, motion, object detection and recognition) from images, videos and 3D sensors like LiDAR, Realsense.

Now, i'am currently working on a project about environment perception (3D object detection and semantic segmentation on point cloud from lidar sensor) for self-driving cars.
